Welcome to Aariya SkillTech Academy

Founded with a vision to empower students with cutting-edge technology skills, Aariya SkillTech Academy has become a leading computer training institute dedicated to shaping successful IT professionals.

Under the leadership of our Managing Director, Mr. Roshan Singh, we have trained thousands of students who have gone on to build remarkable careers in the technology sector.

Our institute combines theoretical knowledge with practical, hands-on experience to ensure that every student is industry-ready upon course completion.

Our Mission

To provide world-class IT education and training that empowers individuals with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in the rapidly evolving technology landscape. We are committed to nurturing talent, fostering innovation, and creating opportunities for our students to achieve their professional goals.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the premier computer training institute that transforms aspiring professionals into industry leaders through excellence in education, innovative teaching methodologies, and strong industry partnerships. We envision a future where every student achieves their full potential.
