Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements?

Basic computer knowledge and a high school diploma are required. Specific courses may have additional prerequisites which will be mentioned in the course details.

Do you provide placement assistance?

Yes, we provide 100% placement assistance to all our students. Our dedicated placement cell works with leading companies to ensure job opportunities for our graduates.

Are the courses certified?

Yes, all our courses are certified. Upon successful completion, you'll receive an industry-recognized certificate that adds value to your resume.

Can I pay the fee in installments?

Yes, we offer flexible payment options including EMI facilities. Contact our admission office for detailed information about payment plans.

What is the batch size for courses?

We maintain small batch sizes of 15-20 students per batch to ensure personalized attention and effective learning.
